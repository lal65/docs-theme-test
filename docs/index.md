## Welcome
Welcome to the technical user guide for
[https://www.worldcampus.psu.edu](https://www.worldcampus.psu.edu), otherwise
known as "The Prospect Site". This guide is a work in progress and will grow
over time.

## What is the prospect site?
The prospect site rests at the very top of the marketing funnel. It is the
primary marketing hub and is the main receiver of all campaign traffic.
Annually this application serves more than 4.1 million users, processes more
than 140000 requests for information, 10500 event registrations, and over 6500
chat sessions with admissions counselors. At any time there are over 100
campaigns simultaneously active.

### As a general page builder
With a carefully curated assortment of page building features, content managers
are able to produce consistent, visually appealing, on-brand, and accessible
content. The concept of "one body field" no longer exists, giving authors the
editorial freedom to create ambitious digital experiences while simultaneously
benefiting from a strict content moderation workflow.

Built-in support for analytics gives strategists in-depth insights into how
users are interacting with the content.

### As a structured data power house
Housing the entire World Campus program and b2b portfolios and enriched by
carefully designed taxonomies, the _Create Once Publish Everywhere_ content
management strategy is heavily utilized. Content is reused and referenced in a
variety of ways, enabling scalability and a truly hands-off approach to
campaign portfolio management.

### As a landing page generator
Campaign landing pages primarily receive paid traffic routed to World Campus
from third party affiliate marketers. These landing pages are largely
open-ended in terms of structure, but still follow an overarching set of
guidelines.

With an integration to the Salesforce CRM system, leads generated from these
campaign pages are fed into the top of the funnel for follow-up correspondence.

### As a form builder
The webform module for Drupal is an enterprise grade form builder. From simple
contact and feedback forms to complex multistep applications, webform can
do it all, even integrating with third party platforms with no code.
